About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization established in 2020. The NGO focuses on education support, food distribution, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, youth development, and social welfare initiatives.

Our Major Projects

Project SEVA

Supporting underprivileged communities through food distribution and essential aid.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Promoting educational awareness and learning opportunities for children.

Project UDAAN

Empowering women through awareness, support, and opportunities.

Project PRAKRITI

Encouraging environmental sustainability and plantation drives.

Project JEEV

Supporting animal welfare and compassion initiatives.

Project VIKAS

Helping youth develop practical skills and leadership qualities.
